# PCA Why learn it

- PCA – for dimensionality reduction - $R^{d}$ to $R^{d^{'}}$ where $d^{'} \ll d$
- It is use for:
	- For visualization (learning now)
	- $d^{'} < d$ (dimensional reduction) for model training (learning after some chapter)
